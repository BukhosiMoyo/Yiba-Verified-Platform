## 1. Purpose
This policy defines how long data is retained, how it is stored, archived, and disposed of, in compliance with POPIA and regulatory best practices.

The policy ensures:
- Protection of personal information
- Availability of records for audits and reviews
- Clear rules for archiving and disposal
- Legal defensibility

---

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all data stored within the system, including:
- Learner personal data
- Enrolment records
- Attendance records
- Assessment and readiness data
- Evidence documents
- Audit logs
- System metadata

---

## 3. Data Classification

### 3.1 Personal Data
Examples:
- National ID / Passport
- Contact details
- Demographic information

### 3.2 Academic & Compliance Data
Examples:
- Enrolments
- Readiness (Form 5)
- Attendance
- Certificates

### 3.3 System & Audit Data
Examples:
- Audit logs
- Change history
- Access logs

---

## 4. Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---------|------------------|
| Learner Personal Data | Minimum 5 years after last activity |
| Enrolment Records | Minimum 10 years |
| Attendance Records | Minimum 10 years |
| Certificates | Permanent |
| Programme Readiness (Form 5) | Minimum 10 years |
| Evidence Documents | Minimum 10 years |
| Audit Logs | Permanent |
| System Logs | Minimum 5 years |

Retention periods may be extended if required by law or regulation.

---

## 5. Archiving Rules
- Data exceeding active use is archived
- Archived data is read-only
- Archived data remains searchable by authorised roles
- Archiving does not remove audit trails

---

## 6. Deletion & Disposal
- No hard deletion of compliance-related data
- Personal data may be anonymised after retention period
- Disposal actions are logged and approved
- Evidence and audit logs are never deleted

---

## 7. Access Control
- Only authorised roles may access archived data
- Student access is limited to active records
- QCTO access includes archived compliance data

---

## 8. Data Subject Rights (POPIA)
- Learners may request access to their data
- Requests for correction follow Change Management Policy
- Requests for deletion are evaluated against legal obligations

---

## 9. Security Measures
- Encrypted storage
- Role-based access
- Secure backups
- Controlled data exports

---

## 10. Policy Review
- Reviewed annually
- Updated as legislation or regulatory requirements change
- Versioned with no retroactive effect
